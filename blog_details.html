Messi made a strong statement on his Paris Saint-Germain debut

  • Admin
  • 12-08-2021
  • No Comments

After the wait of global football fans, the superstar Lionel Messi finally made it to become a Paris Saint-Germain player. The Argentinian striker put pen to paper a two-year contract with an optional extension of 12 months

Right in the ceremony to launch the new team, the captain La Albiceleste showed great determination. El Pulga's goal when moving to Parc des Princes is to have more individual titles as well as collective.

As we reported earlier, the owner of six Golden Ball titles will wear the number 30 shirt at the team new ball. Striker Neymar had the courtesy to give up the number 10 to his close friend, but Messi refused turn down that opportunity to show respect to the Brazilian player.

If there are no surprises, Leo Messi will make his debut for his new team this weekend. The home team The Princes' Park will host Strasbourg in the second round of Ligue 1 at 2:00 am on August 15 (Vietnam time).
